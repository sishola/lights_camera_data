Lights! Camera! Data! is a data visualization project based on 250 of the highest grossing movies of all time according to the amount they grossed over their opening weekend.


Browse the website to learn more about how different variables in the movie industry interact with one another and try to spot some trends among the top 250 films.



Visuals that should help along the way include:

  • A ring diagram that delves into the relationships between films, actors and the movie ratings systems
  • A word cloud that takes a look into the role of genre when it comes to financial success and its frequency
  • A packed bubble chart and a tree map that explore how total gross earnings is broken down on a studio level, and then further on a per movie basis
  • A world map that investigates foreign gross box office


    • Data was sourced from Box Office Mojo and the OMDb API. It was then transformed using Python and loaded into MongoDB. The visualization is powered by Python Flask.


    Visuals were made using d3.js, dex.js, leaflet.js, AnyChart.js, core.js (via AmCharts) and Google Chart.
